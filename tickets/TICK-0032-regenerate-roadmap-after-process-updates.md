# Title

Regenerate roadmap after completed process tickets

## Status

done

## Priority

high

## Type

process

## Summary

Refresh `tickets/ROADMAP.md` after the recently completed process tickets so it only lists currently incomplete work in the right priority buckets.

## Scope

- Remove completed tickets from the roadmap
- Keep incomplete tickets in their existing priority buckets unless intentionally reprioritized

## Out of Scope

- Large-scale backlog reprioritization
- Splitting future tickets

## Acceptance Criteria

- `tickets/ROADMAP.md` lists only incomplete tickets
- Recently completed process tickets are removed from the roadmap

## Notes

- This keeps the roadmap aligned with the current ticket state.
