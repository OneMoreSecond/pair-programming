# Title

Support multiple reviewers and aggregate their results

## Status

todo

## Priority

low

## Type

feature

## Summary

Extend the workflow to support more than one reviewer and combine their outputs into a final decision or summary.

## Scope

- Run multiple reviewer roles against the same round
- Preserve each reviewer result as a separate artifact
- Aggregate reviewer outcomes into a task-level decision model

## Out of Scope

- Complex human approval routing
- Enterprise policy integration

## Acceptance Criteria

- A round can be reviewed by more than one reviewer
- Reviewer outputs remain auditable individually
- The workflow has a documented aggregation rule

## Notes

- This is an advanced policy requirement from the architecture and UX docs.
